Services

Medical consultations at Pulse Well involve one-on-one sessions with our experienced medical professionals. During these consultations, patients discuss their health concerns, receive expert advice, and develop treatment plans.

Diagnostic imaging encompasses a range of cutting-edge technologies, including X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds. These non-invasive procedures capture internal images of the body to aid in the accurate diagnosis of medical conditions

Pulse Well's state-of-the-art laboratories perform a wide array of diagnostic tests, including blood tests, urine analysis, and genetic screening. These tests aid in the precise identification of medical conditions and inform treatment plans.

Our pediatric services are dedicated to the comprehensive care of infants, children, and adolescents. This includes routine check-ups, vaccinations, developmental assessments, and specialized care for pediatric health issues.

Otolaryngology services at Pulse Well focus on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the ears, nose, and throat. This includes addressing issues like hearing problems, sinus infections, and throat conditions.

Pulse Well prioritizes women's health by providing specialized care for gynecological concerns. These services encompass routine gynecological check-ups, prenatal care, and treatment for women's health conditions.

Our urology services focus on addressing urinary and reproductive health issues in both men and women. This includes the diagnosis and treatment of conditions such as urinary tract infections and reproductive disorders.

Proctology services at Pulse Well address conditions related to the rectum and anus. These include the diagnosis and treatment of conditions such as hemorrhoids, anal fissures, and colorectal issues.

Cardiology services at Pulse Well focus on the diagnosis and treatment of heartrelated conditions. This includes monitoring heart health, diagnosing heart diseases, and providing cardiac care.

Gastroenterology services address a wide range of digestive health issues. Specialists in this field diagnose and treat conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome, gastritis, and other gastrointestinal disorders.

Endocrinology services focus on hormonal imbalances, diagnosing and treating conditions related to the endocrine system. This includes managing thyroid disorders, diabetes, and hormonal imbalances.

Beyond medical care, Pulse Well offers physiotherapeutic and balneological procedures to rejuvenate the body and improve overall well-being. These procedures include physical therapy, hydrotherapy, and other therapeutic treatments.
